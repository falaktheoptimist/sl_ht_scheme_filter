Post SSC Scholarship to SEBC students living in Hostels

  • Print this Page
  • Share

Eligibility Criteria

  1. This scheme is Centrally Sponsored
  2. The Central Govt. gives 100% grants for this scheme
  3. Annual Income Limit Rs. 1,50,000/-
  4. For S.E.B.C students studying in std. 11, 12, Graduation and Post Graduation
